About Us

Welcome to Bangalore IAS Academy

Why should you choose Bangalore School of Civil Services.

    Faculty

    Arjun Bopanna team consists of renowned faculties from Delhi, Hyderabad & Bangalore with a teaching experience of more than three years in their specialised subject. They will ensure that all the knowledge is passed on to the students & increase their confidence level within the time line


    Study Materials

    Our institute have our own printed materials for each subject which we provide exclusively to our students. The materials are handy as it covers the entire syllabus & easy to understand. The important points/contents have been marked to ensure students do not miss to read it or to give importance.


    Prelims/Mains Test

    We conduct weekly test on the topics we have covered for all the subjects. Once the test is completed, we discuss the answers with the students which helps them to understand/check their knowledge on the particular topic


    Current Affairs Magazine

    As current affair plays a vital role to crack the exams. We do understand there are possibilities for students to miss out few important topics from the newspaper. To ensure students have the complete knowledge of current affairs, we provide current affair magazine "Mahithi" by NammaKPSC team on monthly basis. It covers all the important topics from State level, National level & International level.


    Library Facility

    We encourage students to utilize the BSCS library. We have good collections of books related to UPSC/KAS exams. Students can avail the library facility & can study in the classroom after the classes.


    Time Table/Schedules

    As planning is very important to clear these exams, we in BSCS team make a timetable/schedules well before in advance & pass it on to students which in turn helps them to plan their preparation for exams.


    Flexible Batches

    We do understand different aspirants are flexible with different timings & to ensure students have this flexibility we do have three batches for UPSC/KAS course. We conduct weekday’s morning batch from 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM & evening batch from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM. We do conduct weekend batch especially for working aspirants, on Saturday from 2:00PM to 6:00 PM & on Sunday from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM


    Place

    The institute is located in a prime location; you have plenty of buses plying from here to different location.


    Fees

    BSCS team ensures that the fees are kept nominal. We do not wish to burden the aspirants with high fees. Please note we do not charge extra for study materials/test series/current affair magazine. It is already included with the fees


    Overall

    BSCS team is completely dedicated towards the serious aspirants. We ensure we do not leave any stones unturned. Here we are focussed on quality of education & to ensure the aspirants gain more knowledge & confidence to crack the exam. We create a healthy atmosphere for our student which helps them to keep up the focus. We have a dedicated team who will help you to plan your studies & provide counselling whenever required. We understand how important is for an aspirant to crack the exam
    To ensure you have taken the right decision by choosing Bangalore School of Civil Services, we encourage aspirants to attend demo class & talk to our students who are already part of BSCS. Once you are completely satisfied you are welcome to join our institute.
